2Know:

Coronavirus Disease (COVID-19)

Overview:

COVID-19, caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus, can lead to mild to severe respiratory illness. It affects all age groups and is best prevented through informed measures like social distancing, mask-wearing, hand hygiene, vaccination, and respiratory etiquette.

Prevention:

1. Get vaccinated when eligible.
2. Maintain 1-meter distance from others.
3. Wear a well-fitted mask indoors or in crowded areas.
4. Prefer well-ventilated spaces and open windows indoors.
5. Practice regular hand hygiene.
6. Cover your mouth and nose when sneezing or coughing.
7. Stay home and self-isolate if unwell.

Symptoms:

COVID-19 symptoms vary widely, with most individuals having a mild to moderate illness. Common signs are fever, cough, tiredness, and loss of taste/smell. Less common symptoms include sore throat, headache, aches, diarrhea, skin rash, and red/irritated eyes. In severe cases, people may experience difficulty breathing, loss of speech/mobility, confusion, or chest pain.

Other Information:

Seek immediate medical attention for serious symptoms, manage mild symptoms at home, and note that symptoms may appear within 5-14 days of infection.
